South Florida Building Safety Study

Initiated by a South Florida firefighter working to improve how property information is organized and shared with first responders.

We are gathering feedback from property owners, managers, and facility teams to understand how properties organize safety and operational information today. Participation takes about 2 minutes.

Informed Consent — South Florida Building Safety Study

Purpose

This study is conducted by SYT.FYI to better understand how property owners, managers, and facility teams organize safety and operational information, and to identify opportunities to improve how that information is shared with first responders.

What Participation Involves

  • Completing a short survey of approximately 6 questions
  • Participation takes roughly 2 minutes
  • You may skip any question you prefer not to answer

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may stop at any time without penalty. There is no compensation for participation.

Confidentiality

Contact information you provide is optional and used solely for follow-up if you request a free SYT.360 Safety Visit. Survey responses are used only for internal research purposes and are not sold or shared with third parties.

Risks

There are no known risks associated with participating in this study. No personally identifiable information is required to complete the survey.

Questions

If you have questions about this study, contact us at info@syt.fyi.
