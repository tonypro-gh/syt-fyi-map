Authorization & Data Handling *
Read each line slowly. Only check after they agree. Ongoing accuracy depends on information provided or confirmed by the property and its authorized representatives.

Updates & reaffirmation: All records reflect conditions as observed and documented at the time of the visit and as subsequently updated by the property or authorized users. Profiles and pages may be reaffirmed periodically (e.g., every 24 months) to confirm accuracy. The property may request updates or removal of specific media at any time. If not reaffirmed, profiles may be archived or marked as not recently reaffirmed.

Withdrawal of Consent: The property may withdraw any authorization granted in this form at any time by providing written notice to SYT.FYI, including by email to info@syt.fyi, or by other written communication. Upon processing a valid withdrawal request, any responder-facing information previously authorized for display through FireTactic.com will be removed from responder access within a reasonable processing period. Withdrawal does not affect materials already generated for the property's internal records, and does not retroactively alter lawful uses made prior to withdrawal.

Once information is shared with third parties, including governmental responders, SYT.FYI and First In Safety Foundation cannot control third-party retention, disclosure, or use.
