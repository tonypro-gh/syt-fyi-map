  • Authorization & Scope: This Intake is completed pursuant to a separately executed SYT.360 Consent for Documentation. The individual providing information represents they are authorized to participate in the walkthrough and provide information as permitted by that Consent.
  • Informational, Observational Only: This Intake records visually observed or property-reported facts and locations only. It is non-invasive, not verified, not real-time, and must not be relied upon for emergency response, life-safety decisions, or regulatory compliance.
  • No Duty or Professional Services: SYT.FYI does not perform inspections, testing, certifications, engineering analysis, code evaluations, or professional services. The property owner/operator remains solely responsible for safety, compliance, maintenance, and information accuracy.
  • Standards & Limits: References to OSHA, NFPA, or other standards are for contextual orientation only and do not represent an evaluation of compliance, adherence, or sufficiency. Conditions not visible or disclosed may not be captured. Sensitive security information is not collected.
  • Sharing & Handling: Any responder visibility is optional, property-controlled, limited, and revocable per Consent.
